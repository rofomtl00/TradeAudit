The Problem

When a platform shows wrong PnL, wrong fees, or wrong fill prices — you make decisions based on bad data. After the fact, you can't prove what the platform displayed.

3Commas (2021)

Dashboard showed profit while users lost money after fees. Users increased position sizes based on phantom gains. No compensation.

Pionex Grid Bots

"Grid profit" excluded unrealized inventory loss. Users thought they were profitable when they were underwater. No compensation.

FTX (2022)

Displayed balances that didn't exist. Users saw numbers on screen that bore no relationship to reality. Billions lost.

Every Other Platform

ToS says "information displayed is for reference only." They legally disclaim their own dashboard. You have no independent record.

How It Works

1

TradeAudit sits between your bot and the exchange

Every API call and response is logged. Your bot connects to localhost:8877 instead of the exchange. Zero code changes needed.

2

Every record is SHA-256 hash-chained

Each row includes the hash of the previous row. Modify any record and the chain breaks from that point forward. Tamper-evident by design.

3

Daily digest for external proof

Once per day, a SHA-256 hash of the entire file is generated. Email it to yourself. This proves the file existed in that exact state on that date.

4

Balance reconciliation catches discrepancies

Every cycle, TradeAudit compares your internal PnL against the exchange-reported balance. Any delta is logged immediately.

What Gets Logged

Decision Inputs

Every indicator value, signal, and filter state at the exact moment a trade decision was made.

Raw Exchange Response

Order ID, filled qty, actual price, fees charged — straight from the exchange API.

PnL Calculation

Entry, exit, fees, funding cost, settlement count — every component of your profit or loss.

Balance Reconciliation

Internal PnL vs exchange balance every cycle. Delta tracked and logged.

Funding Rates

What the exchange reported at the time of query. Proves rate used in your calculations.

Order IDs Linked

Open and close order IDs linked to every trade. Direct audit trail to exchange records.
